Frequently Asked Questions 💖

Everything you need to know about our glosses and ordering

How much are the glosses?
Each gloss is $7 and includes a cute pom‑pom keychain — no extra charge!
Do you deliver?
Yes! We deliver across Grand Bahama. Just let us know your location when you order and we'll arrange delivery for you.
How do I place an order?
It's super easy! Click any "Order Now" button and it will open WhatsApp with a pre-written message. Just hit send and we'll get back to you quickly!
What flavors do you have?
We currently have 5 flavors: Bird of Paradise, Peach, Clear, Watermelon, and Mango. We're always working on new flavors — stay tuned!
Do the glosses have scents?
Yes! Every gloss has a sweet scent that matches its flavor perfectly. They smell amazing!
Do they come with pom‑pom keychains?
Absolutely — every single gloss includes a cute pom‑pom keychain as part of the package. It's our signature! 🎀
How long does delivery take?
Usually the same day or next day depending on your location on Grand Bahama. We try to be as fast as possible!
Can I order more than one?
Of course! Order as many as you want. Mix and match different flavors too — they make great gifts!
How do I pay?
We accept cash on delivery. Just pay when your gloss arrives at your door!
Can I buy as a gift?
Yes! Our glosses make amazing gifts. Just let us know when ordering and we can arrange delivery to a different address.
