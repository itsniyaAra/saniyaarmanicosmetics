About Saniya Armani Cosmetics

A Young Bahamian Beauty Brand

Saniya Armani Cosmetics was created to bring confidence, shine, and personality to every girl’s beauty routine. What started as a small idea grew into a brand known for glossy lips, fluffy pom-pom keychains, and scents that make you feel cute, bold, and unforgettable.

Every gloss is chosen with care and packaged with love. We believe beauty should be affordable, fun, and full of personality — just like you.
